# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Foam factory managers and employees use this CRM on desktop and mobile while handling orders, production scheduling, delivery signing, cost entry, approvals, and customer payment follow-up. Mobile users are often on the shop floor, in storage areas, or at delivery handoff, so screens must be quick to scan and safe to operate with one hand.

## Product Purpose

The product centralizes foam factory order operations from customer order intake through production, delivery, reconciliation, payment, and cost tracking. Success means employees can record work in the moment, managers can see reliable operational status, and desktop/mobile data stays synchronized without exposing data to unassigned users.

## Brand Personality

Professional, calm, practical. The interface should feel like a dependable operations tool: clear enough for fast daily use, polished enough for management, and restrained enough to keep production data readable.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative dashboards, dense dark admin panels on mobile, over-styled cards, tiny tap targets, unclear status colors, and layouts that hide primary work actions behind unnecessary modals or visual noise.

## Design Principles

Make the next operational action obvious on every screen.

Use familiar iOS product patterns for navigation, forms, cards, sheets, and touch targets.

Keep visual hierarchy calm: data first, decoration last.

Protect business data by making pending authorization states explicit and empty until roles are assigned.

Design for shop-floor conditions: high contrast, generous spacing, and clear status language.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target readable contrast for all production data and form controls. Keep touch targets at least 44px high, avoid relying on color alone for status meaning, and keep motion minimal so the app remains usable in busy work environments.
